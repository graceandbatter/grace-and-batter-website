About Grace and Batter

Grace and Batter was founded by Miracle Gardner with a passion for creating handcrafted desserts that bring people together.

As a faith-based bakery, every treat is baked with intention, quality ingredients, and a commitment to serving others with excellence.

Our mission is simple: to create desserts that feel homemade, meaningful, and memorable while sharing God's love through every order.

Baked with Grace. Served with Love.
